NATIONAL GENOMIC INTELLIGENCE

GeneAtlas

A secure national platform for synthetic genomic datasets, AI analysis, and privacy-preserving genomic computing

Executive Summary

GeneAtlas is a secure, government-controlled national genomic platform designed to store citizens’ full DNA sequences and unlock transformative applications through advanced artificial intelligence.

The foundational infrastructure for precision medicine, genomic understanding, and informed life decisions in the 21st century.

VISION

A unified, privacy-first DNA registry enabling proactive healthcare, data-driven governance, and scientific leadership.

Core Concept

A centralized, highly secure BioVault where governments register genomic sequences with consent, and individuals control access to their own data.
