# Government Registration & User Access Flow

## 1. Government DNA Registration Process

When a citizen receives their national ID card or when a baby is born, the government will:

- Take a blood sample (or other appropriate biological sample)
- Extract the person's **full DNA code** (whole genome sequencing)
- Save the complete DNA sequence in a highly secure national database
- Link the DNA record with the person's national ID, personal information, and biometric data
- Store the data under strict encryption and privacy protocols

This creates a universal, secure genomic registry for all citizens from birth.

---

## 2. User Mobile Application

Every citizen can install the official **GeneAtlas** mobile application on their smartphone.

After secure identity verification (biometric + national ID), the user gains access to their personal genomic profile.

### Information Available to the User in the App:

1. **Full DNA Code**  
   View and (optionally) download their complete raw genomic sequence.

2. **DNA-Based Health Insights**  
   Comprehensive report of potential genetic illnesses, disease risks, and carrier status.

3. **Biological Relatives**  
   Automatically detected biological parents, children, and other relatives found in the national database through AI-powered DNA matching.

4. **Dynamic Family Tree**  
   Interactive visual family tree showing all detected biological relationships across generations.

5. **Phenotypic Reconstruction**  
   AI-generated visualization of the user's body and facial features (shape, size, type, skin tone, hair type, etc.) based on their DNA.

6. **Advanced Trait Analysis**  
   Genetic insights regarding:
   - Estimated IQ range and cognitive abilities
   - Natural talents and aptitudes (sports, music, mathematics, arts, etc.)
   - Body composition tendencies
   - Brain structure and neurological features
   - Other genetically influenced characteristics

7. **Similarity Matching**  
   Compare DNA with famous or historically significant individuals to see genetic similarities.

8. **Pre-Marriage / Family Planning Tool**  
   - Users can send a secure genetic compatibility request to their partner through the app
   - Partner receives and accepts the request
   - AI analyzes both genomes and provides detailed predictions about:
     - Possible health outcomes for future children
     - Genetic probabilities and trait combinations
     - Estimated chances of high intelligence, specific talents, or other desired characteristics
   - Results are shown privately to both parties

---

## Additional Notes

- All features are **opt-in** and respect user privacy.
- Sensitive analyses (especially family planning and trait prediction) require explicit consent from all involved parties.
- The system is designed to be secure, ethical, and transparent.
