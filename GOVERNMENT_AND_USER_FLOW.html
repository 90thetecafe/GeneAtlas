Government & User System Flow

How GeneAtlas Works

🏛️ 1. Government Registration Process

When issuing a national ID card or when a baby is born, the government will:

  • 01 Take a blood sample (or appropriate biological sample)
  • 02 Extract the person's full DNA code (whole genome sequencing)
  • 03 Securely save the complete DNA sequence in the national database
  • 04 Link it with the person's national ID and personal information

All data is stored with military-grade encryption and strict legal protections.

📱 2. User Mobile Application

Citizens install the official GeneAtlas app and access their personal genomic profile after secure verification.

1. Full DNA Code

View and optionally download your complete raw genomic sequence.

2. DNA Illnesses & Risks

Comprehensive genetic health report including disease risks and carrier status.

3. Biological Family

See detected biological parents, children, and relatives found by AI in the national database.

4. Interactive Family Tree

Dynamic visual family tree showing all biological relationships.

5. Body & Face Reconstruction

AI-generated visualization of your facial features, body type, shape, and physical characteristics based on DNA.

6. Advanced Genetic Traits

Insights about IQ range, natural talents, sports aptitude, cognitive features, and more.

7. Celebrity DNA Matching

Compare your DNA with famous individuals and see genetic similarities.

8. Partner Compatibility & Baby Prediction

Send a secure request to your partner → AI calculates genetic possibilities for future children (health, traits, IQ potential, talents, etc.) and shows results to both parties.
